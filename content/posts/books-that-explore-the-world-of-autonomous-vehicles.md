Autonomous vehicles are no longer just a concept of the future; they are becoming increasingly prevalent in today's world. From self-driving cars to delivery drones, the realm of autonomous vehicles is rapidly evolving and reshaping the way we think about transportation. If you're curious to learn more about this exciting field, here are five books that delve into the world of autonomous vehicles:

### 1. "Autonomy: The Quest to Build the Driverless Carâ€”And How It Will Reshape Our World" by Lawrence D. Burns and Christopher Shulgan

In this insightful book, Lawrence D. Burns, a former vice president of research and development at General Motors, provides a detailed account of the history and future of autonomous vehicles. From the challenges of developing self-driving technology to the potential societal impacts of widespread adoption, "Autonomy" offers a comprehensive look at the driving forces behind the autonomous vehicle revolution.

### 2. "Driverless: Intelligent Cars and the Road Ahead" by Hod Lipson and Melba Kurman

"Driverless" explores the cutting-edge technologies that are driving the development of autonomous vehicles. Authors Hod Lipson and Melba Kurman delve into the world of artificial intelligence, robotics, and machine learning to explain how self-driving cars are poised to revolutionize the transportation industry. This book provides a fascinating look at the opportunities and challenges presented by autonomous vehicles.

### 3. "Autonomous Driving: How the Driverless Revolution Will Change the World" by Andreas Herrmann, Walter Brenner, and Rupert Stadler

For those interested in the business and societal implications of autonomous vehicles, "Autonomous Driving" offers a comprehensive overview of the transformative potential of self-driving technology. The authors explore topics such as mobility services, urban planning, and regulatory frameworks to paint a holistic picture of how autonomous vehicles will reshape our world.

### 4. "Human + Machine: Reimagining Work in the Age of AI" by Paul R. Daugherty and H. James Wilson

While not solely focused on autonomous vehicles, "Human + Machine" delves into the broader impact of artificial intelligence on society and the workforce. The book explores how AI and automation are changing the nature of work and offers insights into how humans and machines can collaborate effectively. Understanding the intersection of AI and autonomous vehicles is crucial for grasping the full implications of this technological revolution.

### 5. "The Driver in the Driverless Car: How Our Technology Choices Will Create the Future" by Vivek Wadhwa and Alex Salkever

In "The Driver in the Driverless Car," authors Vivek Wadhwa and Alex Salkever examine the ethical and moral dilemmas posed by autonomous vehicles. From questions of liability and safety to concerns about job displacement, this book tackles the complex issues surrounding self-driving technology. By exploring the human side of autonomous vehicles, the authors prompt readers to consider the broader societal implications of this transformative technology.

Whether you're an industry professional, a technology enthusiast, or simply curious about the future of transportation, these books offer valuable insights into the world of autonomous vehicles. Dive into these captivating reads to gain a deeper understanding of the technology, challenges, and opportunities associated with self-driving cars and other autonomous vehicles. Happy reading!
