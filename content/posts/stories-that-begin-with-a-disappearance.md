When a story starts with a disappearance, readers are immediately drawn into a suspenseful and captivating narrative. The mystery surrounding the missing person, the unanswered questions, and the twists and turns along the way make for an intriguing and compelling read. In this blog post, we have curated a list of five must-read stories that begin with a disappearance, guaranteed to keep you on the edge of your seat from start to finish.

### 1. "Gone Girl" by Gillian Flynn

"Gone Girl" is a psychological thriller that kicks off with the sudden disappearance of Amy Dunne on her fifth wedding anniversary. As the search for Amy unfolds, secrets and lies are exposed, leading to a thrilling and twisted tale of deception, manipulation, and revenge. Flynn's masterful storytelling keeps readers guessing until the shocking conclusion, making this novel a must-read for fans of dark and gripping mysteries.

### 2. "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" by Stieg Larsson

In "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo," investigative journalist Mikael Blomkvist is hired to solve the decades-old disappearance of Harriet Vanger, a member of the powerful Vanger family. Teaming up with the enigmatic hacker Lisbeth Salander, Blomkvist delves into a complex web of family secrets, corruption, and violence. Larsson's intricate plot and compelling characters make this novel a thrilling and unforgettable read that explores the dark side of human nature.

### 3. "Big Little Lies" by Liane Moriarty

"Big Little Lies" begins with the mysterious death of a parent at a school trivia night, setting off a chain of events that unravel the secrets and scandals within a seemingly idyllic community. As the story unfolds, the disappearance of a pivotal character adds layers of intrigue and tension to the narrative. Moriarty's sharp wit, insightful observations, and skillful storytelling make this novel a gripping and thought-provoking exploration of friendship, motherhood, and the consequences of keeping secrets.

### 4. "Before I Go to Sleep" by S.J. Watson

"Before I Go to Sleep" follows Christine Lucas, a woman with amnesia who wakes up every day with no memory of her past. As she pieces together fragments of her life, she discovers that her husband is keeping secrets from her, including the truth about a past disappearance. Watson's taut and suspenseful writing keeps readers guessing as Christine unravels the mystery of her missing memories and confronts the shocking truth about her past.

### 5. "Sharp Objects" by Gillian Flynn

In "Sharp Objects," journalist Camille Preaker returns to her hometown to cover the murders of two young girls, triggering memories of her own troubled past. As Camille investigates the killings, she uncovers dark secrets and a connection to the disappearance of her half-sister years ago. Flynn's haunting prose and complex characters create a chilling and atmospheric story that delves into the psychological trauma of the past and the devastating impact of family secrets.

These five gripping stories that begin with a disappearance are sure to captivate and enthrall readers with their suspenseful plots, complex characters, and unexpected twists. Whether you enjoy psychological thrillers, dark mysteries, or compelling dramas, these novels are guaranteed to keep you guessing until the very end. So, grab a copy of one of these books, settle in, and prepare to be immersed in a world of secrets, suspense, and intrigue. Happy reading!
